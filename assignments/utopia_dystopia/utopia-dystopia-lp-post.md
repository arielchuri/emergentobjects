# Utopia/Dystopia Learning Portfolio Post

**Week:** 1 (Asynchronous)
**Due:** Week 2 (Feb 2)
**Weight:** Part of Persona & User Journey (10%)

## Learning Outcomes

This assignment maps to the following learning outcomes:

- **LO 5 - Broad Application:** Use speculative scenarios to inspire creative problem-solving across art, design, and strategic thinking fields.
- **LO 6 - Life:** Reflect on personal responsibility and self-understanding through the creation of objects that respond to a utopian or dystopian future.
- **LO 7 - Reflection:** Document their process and reflect on their learning via the Parsons Learning Portfolio.

## Description

Create a Learning Portfolio post exploring utopian and dystopian futures. This assignment introduces you to speculative design thinking and sets the foundation for your final emergent object project.

## Requirements

- Research and analyze examples of utopian and/or dystopian scenarios in design, literature, film, or other media
- Reflect on how these speculative futures relate to current societal values and technological trends
- Document your thoughts and initial ideas in your Parsons Learning Portfolio
- Include visual references and examples
- Write 300-500 words reflecting on what kind of future scenario interests you most and why

## Deliverables

- A complete Learning Portfolio post with:
  - Written reflection (300-500 words)
  - Visual references (minimum 3 images)
  - Initial thoughts on potential project directions
