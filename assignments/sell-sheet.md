# Sell Sheet

**Week:** Assigned Week 5, Due Week 6
**Due:** Mar 9
**Weight:** Part of Object Sketches & Presentation (10%)

## Learning Outcomes

This assignment maps to the following learning outcomes:

- **LO 5 - Broad Application:** Use speculative scenarios to inspire creative problem-solving across art, design, and strategic thinking fields.
- **LO 7 - Reflection:** Document their process and reflect on their learning via the Parsons Learning Portfolio.

## Description

Create a one-page "sell sheet" that presents your emergent object concept as if it were a real product in your speculative future. This document should communicate your vision clearly and persuasively.

## Requirements

- Design a single-page document that includes:
  - Product name and tagline
  - Hero image or illustration of your object
  - Key features and benefits (3-5 bullet points)
  - Target user (your persona)
  - Brief description of how it works (100-150 words)
  - Context within your utopian/dystopian scenario
- Professional layout and visual design
- Clear, compelling copy

## Deliverables

- One-page sell sheet (PDF format)
- Print quality (300 dpi minimum)
- Upload to Canvas and be prepared to present in class

### Design Considerations
- **Typography:** Readable, appropriate, hierarchical
- **Color:** Cohesive palette that supports concept
- **Images:** High quality, relevant, well-integrated
- **White space:** Used effectively for clarity
- **Brand identity:** Consistent visual language

### Assessment Focus

- **Speculative product marketing:** Selling a concept from the future
