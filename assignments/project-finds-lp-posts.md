# Project Find LP Post

This assignment will be ongoing and due every week.

Create a post about a project, tutorial, or article you find interesting and applicable to the class.

The post should include an image, a link to the source. It may include, why it is interesting, important parts, which language and microcontroller, what the inputs and outputs are and more.
